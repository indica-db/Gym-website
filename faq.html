Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 6 AM to 10 PM every day.

Do I need to book a class in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking classes in advance to secure your spot, especially for popular classes.

What should I bring to my first class?

Bring a water bottle, a towel, and wear comfortable workout clothes. If you have your own yoga mat or equipment, feel free to bring that as well!

Do you offer personal training sessions?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions tailored to your fitness goals. You can book a session with one of our certified trainers.

What types of memberships do you offer?

We offer various membership options, including monthly, quarterly, and annual plans. You can choose the one that best fits your needs.

Is there a cancellation policy for classes?

Yes, we have a 24-hour cancellation policy. If you need to cancel a class, please do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid being charged.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for a maximum of three months. Please contact our front desk for more details.

Do you have facilities for showers and changing?

Yes, we have clean and spacious changing rooms with showers available for all members.

Are there age restrictions for gym access?

Members must be at least 16 years old to use the gym facilities. Those under 18 must have parental consent.

Do you offer group classes?

Yes, we offer a variety of group classes, including yoga, pilates, spin, and HIIT. Check our class schedule for more details.

What if I have a medical condition?

If you have any medical conditions or concerns, please consult with your doctor before starting any exercise program. Our trainers can also help modify workouts to suit your needs.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the contact form on our website or by calling our front desk during business hours.
